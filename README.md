## Ops:
Here is a list of all the mathematical functions and formula names used in the code:

1. **Addition** (`+`)
2. **Subtraction** (`-`)
3. **Multiplication** (`*`)
4. **Division** (`/`)
5. **Modulo (Remainder)** (`%`)
6. **Integer Division** (`//`)
7. **Exponentiation** (`**`)
8. **Square** (`x²` or `**2`)
9. **Cube** (`x³` or `**3`)
10. **Nth Power** (`x^n` or `**n`)
11. **Inverse Power** (`x⁻¹` or `**(-1)`)
12. **Square Root** (`²√` or `**(1/2)`)
13. **Cube Root** (`³√` or `**(1/3)`)
14. **Nth Root** (`√` or `**(1/n)`)
15. **Factorial** (`x!` or `factorial(n)`)
16. **Absolute Value** (`abs`)
17. **Percentage** (`%`)
18. **Euler's Number** (`e`)
19. **Exponential** (`e^x`)
20. **Pi** (`π`)
21. **Sine** (`sin`)
22. **Cosine** (`cos`)
23. **Tangent** (`tan`)
24. **Cotangent** (`cot`)
25. **Logarithm (Base 10)** (`log10` or `log`)
26. **Natural Logarithm (Base e)** (`ln`)
